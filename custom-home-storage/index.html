The Maximus Standard

What Actually Makes Storage Custom

"Custom storage" should mean it's built for your exact room, not assembled from a box of pre-cut panels. Every Maximus Closets project starts with an in-home 3D design of your actual space, not a generic template stretched to fit.

01

The 3D Design Process

A designer visits your home, measures the exact space, and builds a 3D model of your pantry, office, or garage before any material is cut. You see and approve the finished layout, finish, and hardware on screen — no surprises on install day.

02

Materials That Actually Hold Up

Premium wood laminates and soft-close hardware as standard, not the particle-board kits sold at big-box stores. Finish options are selected during your 3D design consultation, not after a rushed showroom visit.

03

Our Own Installers, Not Subcontractors

The same local team that designs your project installs it — we don't dispatch a rotating crew of subcontractors like the national franchises do. One point of accountability, start to finish.

04

Pricing You See Before You Sign

A firm, itemized quote after your 3D design is finalized — not an estimate that grows once installation starts. Licensed in New Jersey (HIC #13VH–XXXXXXX) and PA (#XXXXXX), fully insured.

Common Questions

Home Storage FAQ

How much does custom home storage cost?

Most custom home storage projects — pantries, home offices, garage systems, and mudrooms — range from $2,500 to $10,000 installed, depending on size and materials. Every project starts with an in-home 3D design so you get an exact price before any work begins.

How long does installation take?

Most home storage projects are installed in one to two days once your design is finalized. Larger rooms like garages or media walls can take up to three days. Our own installers — never subcontractors — keep scheduling tight.

What materials do you build with?

Premium wood laminates and soft-close hardware as standard, with wood-look, matte, and high-gloss finish options — all selected during your 3D design consultation.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes — every installation is backed by a workmanship warranty, and materials carry their manufacturer warranty.

Do you serve my area?

We build custom home storage across New Jersey and Pennsylvania, including Mercer County NJ and Bucks County PA. See our Locations page for the full list of towns.
